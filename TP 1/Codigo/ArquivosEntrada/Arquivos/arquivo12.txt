Herbicide Potion
Standard Ingredient Measurements; Elderberry Juice; Castor Oil; Asphodel Powder; Ginger Root.
Heat the water in the cauldron until it begins to boil; Add 2 Standard Ingredient Measurements; Add 4 drops of elderberry juice; Add 3 tablespoons of castor oil; Add a pinch of asphodel powder; Stir the mixture 3 times counterclockwise; Add a ginger root cut into small pieces; Let it boil for 10 minutes and then remove from heat; Allow the potion to cool before using it.
