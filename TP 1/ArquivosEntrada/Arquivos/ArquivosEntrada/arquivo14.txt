Agility Potion
Standard Ingredient Measures; Pinch of Unicorn Horns; Pearl Powder; Ginger Root.
Add 2 measures of Standard Ingredient to the cauldron; Add 1 pinch of unicorn horns; Stir the mixture until it is smooth; Add 2 tablespoons of pearl powder; Add a ginger root cut into small pieces; Heat over medium heat for 15 minutes; Allow the potion to cool before using.
