Cure for Boils
Snake Fangs; Horned Slugs; Porcupine Quills.
Add 6 snake fangs to the mortar; Crush into a fine powder; Add 4 measures of the crushed fangs to the cauldron; Heat the mixture to 250 for 10 seconds; Wave your wand; Let it brew and come back in 33-45 minutes; Add 4 horned slugs to the cauldron; Remove the cauldron from the fire before adding the next ingredient; Add 2 porcupine quills to your cauldron; Stir 5 times clockwise. Wave your wand to complete the potion; If the potion is successfully brewed, there will be pink smoke rising from the cauldron.
