Draught of Peace
Water; Standard Ingredient Measurements; Opal Powder; Valerian Syrup; Moonstone Powder; Unicorn Horn Powder.
Heat the water in the cauldron until it begins to boil; Add 3 Standard Ingredient Measurements; Add 2 tablespoons of opal powder and stir until completely dissolved; Add 4 drops of valerian syrup; Add a pinch of moonstone powder; Stir the mixture 3 times counterclockwise; Add 1 teaspoon of unicorn horn powder; Let it boil for 5 minutes and then remove from heat; Allow the potion to cool before storing.
