Amortentia Potion
Standard Ingredient Measurements; Cedarwood Ash; Peppermint Leaf; Pearl Powder; Rose Water.
Heat the water in the cauldron until it begins to boil; Add 3 Standard Ingredient Measurements; Add a pinch of cedarwood ash; Add a peppermint leaf; Stir the mixture until it begins to emit a spiral steam; Add 2 tablespoons of pearl powder; Add 3 drops of rose water; Stir the mixture 5 times clockwise; Let it boil for 7 minutes and then remove from heat; Allow the potion to cool before storing.
