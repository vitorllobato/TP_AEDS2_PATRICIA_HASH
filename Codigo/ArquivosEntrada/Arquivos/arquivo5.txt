Wiggenweld Potion
Salamander Blood; Lionfish Spines; Blindworm Mucus; Honey Water; Berry Juice.
Add salamander blood to the cauldron; Stir until the potion turns orange; Heat until it turns yellow; Add five lionfish spines; Add blindworm mucus until the potion turns purple; Stir until it turns red; Add honey water until it turns turquoise again; Add a few more drops of berry juice; Stir the potion again and let it boil for thirty minutes; Remove the potion from the heat and let it cool. Once cooled, it is ready to use.
