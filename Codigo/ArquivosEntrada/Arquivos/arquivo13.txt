Healing Potion
Standard Ingredient Measurements; Dandelion Juice; Pinch of Unicorn Horn; Powdered Dragonfly Wing Root.
Add 3 Standard Ingredient Measurements to the cauldron; Add 2 tablespoons of dandelion juice; Add 1 pinch of unicorn horn; Stir the mixture until it begins to emit a faint glow; Add 1 teaspoon of powdered dragonfly wing root; Heat on low for 10 minutes; Allow the potion to cool before using.
