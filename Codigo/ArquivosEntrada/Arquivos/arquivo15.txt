Strengthening Solution
Standard Ingredient Measurements; Pinch of Unicorn Horn; Dragon's Blood; Dragon Bone Powder.
Add 3 Standard Ingredient Measurements to the cauldron; Add 1 pinch of unicorn horn; Stir the mixture until it begins to emit a faint glow; Add 2 tablespoons of dragon's blood; Add 1 teaspoon of dragon bone powder; Heat over high heat for 5 minutes; Allow the potion to cool before using.
