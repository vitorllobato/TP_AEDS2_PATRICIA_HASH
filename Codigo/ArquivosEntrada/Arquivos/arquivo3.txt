Antidote for Love Potion
Wiggentree Twigs; Castor Oil; Squill Bulb Extract.
Add four Wiggentree twigs until the potion turns green; Stir until the potion turns orange; Add castor oil until the potion turns blue; Stir until the potion turns purple; Add Squill Bulb Extract until the potion turns red; Add seven Wiggentree twigs; Let it boil until it turns pink.
