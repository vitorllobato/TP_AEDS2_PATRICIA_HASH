Skele-Gro
Powdered Bicorn Horn; Mandrake Root; Boomslang Skin; Salamander Blood; Essence of Murtlap Extract.
Add the powdered bicorn horn to the cauldron and heat over medium flame; Add the mandrake root and mix well; Add the boomslang skin and salamander blood; Let it boil until the mixture turns emerald green; Add the essence of murtlap extract; The potion will be ready when it starts to emit a faint glow.
