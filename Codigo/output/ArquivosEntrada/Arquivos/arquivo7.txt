Potion of Fire Protection
Mushrooms; Salamander Blood; Wart Powder.
Cut the mushrooms and crush them with a knife, add to the cauldron and stir clockwise until the potion turns blue; Add salamander blood to the cauldron, stir counter-clockwise until the potion turns green; Crush the wart powder in the mortar, add to the cauldron and stir clockwise until the potion turns red.
